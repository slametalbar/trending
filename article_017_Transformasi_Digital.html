Terakhir diperbarui: 18 April 2025

The Ministry of Digital (Malay: Kementerian Digital) is a ministry of the Government of Malaysia established on December 12, 2023, to spearhead the nation's digital transformation and competitiveness. Formed through the restructuring of the Ministry of Communications and Digital, it integrates public sector digitization functions, previously managed by MAMPU, now known as the National Digital Department (JDN). The ministry oversees key areas, including digitalisation, personal data protection, cybersecurity, digital economy, digital infrastructure, and domain management. It operates through two main sectors, seven divisions, and supervises two departments, JDN and JPDP & five agencies: MDEC, MyDIGITAL Corporation, CyberSecurity Malaysia (CSM), Digital Nasional Berhad (DNB), and MYNIC Berhad.
